Here are some quotes by notable women in STEM

  2. "Never be limited by other people’s limited imaginations."
    - Mae C. Jemison

  3. "Life is a series of building, testing, changing and iterating."

  4. "Don’t be afraid of hard work. Nothing worthwhile come easily. Don’t let others discourage you or tell you that you can’t do it."

  5. "I learned to always take on things I’d never done before. Growth and comfort do not coexist."

  6. "The time is long overdue to encourage more women to dream the possible dream."

  7. "Dreams do come true, but not without the help of others, a good education, a strong work ethic, and the courage to lean in."

  9. "Learn to ask for things. Be concise, relevant and brave."

  10. "If you find something you feel passionately about, pursue it with all your heart, and don’t let anyone tell you that you’re too young or inexperienced to make it happen."
    - Lauren Kulokas
